ME

Hello! I am a second-year undergrad at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay(IIT-B). I am majoring in physics.

My broad research interest is the philosophical implications and practical applications of quantum theory. This takes me to Quantum Chemistry, Quantum Information Theory, Quantum Computation and other domains that I probably haven't heard of yet! The history of science is another of my interest. And programming - I love it.
